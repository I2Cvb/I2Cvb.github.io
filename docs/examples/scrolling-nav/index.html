Objectives

The Initiative for Collaborative Computer Vision Benchmarking (I2CVB) is devoted to broaden the trustworthy and worthy practises from the Free Software community into the research society.

Facilitate to individuals and groups to research on data collected, mantained and shared by the community.

What

Open data; method evaluation; comparison framework; reporting platform.

From the past decades and the immersion of a digital world, recurrent issues appear in the research society. The I2CVB will focus mainly on addressing the problems related with data collection, unification, evaluation and reporting.

Whaterver the field of research, numerous research works already highlighted the lack of public dataset and/or the avaibility of common evaluation tools. In that regard, the I2CVB will provide open dataset but also common evaluation methods in the different

Community; research groups and individuals from all walks of life.
