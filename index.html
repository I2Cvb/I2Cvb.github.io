What

Open data; evaluation methods; comparison framework; reporting platform.

The lack of common data combined with non-aggregated assessing strategies result in nonexistent or misleading comparisons, which difficult to acknowledge relevant novel methodologies. A common goal for the research and development communities is to overcome such limitations, which can be successfully addressed by co-creation and collaboration.

I2CVB aims at serving as foundation for collecting and sharing data as well as providing common evaluation methodologies. Furthermore, a fair comparison is only achieved by using common data and evaluation techniques.

Who

Community; research groups and individuals from all walks of life.

Something here

How

Transfering successful practises from Free Software and Quality Management

At I2CVB, we like to challenge the impossible along with the status quo therefore we strive to settle a multi-skilled community pursuing common goals to achieve excellence through collaborative continuous improvement.

At I2CVB, we belive that Free Software and Quality Management have already reshaped the world and that is time to apply some of the successful practices learned in such domains to expand the boundaries of research in computer vision and specially for the medical imaging case.
